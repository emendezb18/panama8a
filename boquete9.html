Boquete

The Retirement Paradise

This small mountain town is located in the Chiquiri province in Panama, it is an extremely scenic town situated in the highlands and base of the Baru volcano. The town is a fairly popular retirement spot amongst foreigners, especially Americans and Europeans due to their cool climate, aidback, low-cost of life, surrounded by abundant wildlife, their flora rich of colored orchids, and fertile farmland. It is a vibrant town and is famous as well for its coffee plantations and flowers that animate the town, and the numerous greenhouses.

Due to its fame as a great spot for retirees, boquete brings thousands of tourists looking for a home each year, in addition to this, the festival de las flores y el cafe which has positioned itself as one of the most important festivals in the country takes place in the town. The volcan baru is also visible from the town and a large number of hiking paths with beautiful scenery.
